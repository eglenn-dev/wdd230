Site Name

The site name is going to be: Calgary Chamber of Commerce

This was chosen because it is simple the name of the city in which it operates is first. A short hand for this title can also be CCC.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site will to create a public page view to show the Calgary Chamber of Commerce and the associated local businesses that come together to advocate and support each other and to promote community engagement through an information hub.

Scenarios

  • How can I start a business in Calgary?

  • Are there any upcoming business expos or networking events?

  • Does the chamber offer any discounts or benefits for members?

  • How is commerce expected to change in Calgary over the next 10 years?

Color Schema

Below are the following color choices for the website.

C1: #d3d0cb
C2: #9fb1bc
C3: #6e8898
C4: #2e5266
C5: #e2c044

Each color will be used to help bring the branding and trademark into that page. With the unique accent of C5 it will make our site and brand unique and recognizable.

Typography
