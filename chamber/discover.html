About Calgary

  • Population (metropolitan area, 2021): 1,481,806
  • Area: 825.29 km² (318.65 sq mi)
  • Elevation: 1,042.4 m (3,420 ft) downtown
  • Climate: Subarctic (Köppen: Dfc) with warm summers and cold, snowy winters
  • Nicknames: The Stampede City, Cowtown
  • Major industries: Energy, finance, agriculture, tourism
