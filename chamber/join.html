Membership Levels

Bronze $60/month

Our Bronze membership is perfect for small businesses and non-profits. It includes access to our networking events, business resources, and a listing in our directory.

Silver $80/month

Our Silver membership is perfect for medium-sized businesses. It includes access to our networking events, business resources, and a listing in our directory. It also includes access to our advocacy team.

Gold $100/month

Our Gold membership is perfect for large businesses. It includes access to our networking events, business resources, and a listing in our directory. It also includes access to our advocacy team and a seat on our board of directors.

Non-Profit $0/month

No membership fee is charged for non-profit organizations.
